# UK Business rates 2026

Business rates are a property tax paid by companies for the spaces they occupy. Typically charged at 40-50% of a properties rent, they are a consequential fixed cost for businesses. Two changes are happening to business rates in April 2026: properties are being revalued for the first time in 3 years and the 'multiplier' is changing. The combined effect of these changes will vary for companies based in different places and operating in different industries.
